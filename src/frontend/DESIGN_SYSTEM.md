## 3. Typography
*   **Display Headers**: `Manrope` (Weight: 600-800).
    *   *Usage*: "WANDERLY EXPLORER"
    *   *Styling*: All-caps with `tracking-[0.2em]` for logo/hero.
*   **Body Copy**: `Inter` (Weight: 400, 500, 600).
    *   *Usage*: General content, descriptions.
*   **Serif Accents**: `Instrument Serif` (Italic).
    *   *Usage*: "Moments that matter"

---

## 4. UI Components

### Button Styles
1.  **Primary (The Discovery Action)**:
    *   `bg-teal-600`, `text-white`, `rounded-2xl`, `font-bold`
    *   *Hover*: Background shifts to `teal-700` or `indigo-600` depending on context.
2.  **Ghost (The Support Action)**:
    *   `border border-gray-200`, `text-primary`, `rounded-2xl`
    *   *Hover*: Subtle shadow, border darkens to primary charcoal.

### Card Styles
*   **The "Explorer" Card**:
    *   `rounded-[2.5rem]`, `bg-white`, `border border-gray-100`, `shadow-[0_20px_40px_-10px_rgba(0,0,0,0.03)]`
    *   Padding: `p-6` or `p-8` for balanced spacing.
    *   Images: High-quality Unsplash photography with `object-cover`.

### Spacing System
*   **Standard Section**: `py-24` md:`py-32`
*   **Content Gap**: `gap-10` or `gap-12`
*   **Container Padding**: `px-6` md:`px-12`
